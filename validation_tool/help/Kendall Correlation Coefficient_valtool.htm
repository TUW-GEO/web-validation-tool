Kendall’s Tau

The Kendall’s Tau Correlation Coefficient   computes the monotonic relationship between two time series   and , based on their respective rank.

A pair is called concordant, if both members of one pair are larger than their counterparts in the other pair. It is called discordant, if each pair has one of the larger values.
